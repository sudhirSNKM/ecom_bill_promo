How it works

From product setup to intelligent restocking in four steps.

1
Add your products
Import via CSV or add products manually. Define variants (size, color), set prices, and assign barcodes or SKUs. All products are synced instantly across branches and your online store.
2
Set stock levels per branch
Assign initial stock quantities to each branch. Set your low-stock thresholds so Zenith knows when to alert you.
3
Sell and stock updates automatically
Every time a cashier completes a sale or a customer checks out online, inventory is decremented in real time. Returns are restocked automatically.
4
Get alerts and act fast
When stock drops below your threshold, Zenith alerts you. You can initiate a supplier reorder or a branch transfer directly from the alert without leaving the app.
