How we handle threats

When security events occur, we follow a defined playbook to detect, contain, notify, and remediate.

🔍Detection
Our monitoring systems alert on anomalous API behavior, login patterns, and unusual data access in real time. Average detection time: under 3 minutes.
🚧Containment
Affected accounts or services are isolated within minutes. Automated rate limiting and session invalidation are triggered immediately upon detection.
📣Notification
Affected customers are notified within 72 hours as required by GDPR. Our security team provides regular updates throughout the incident lifecycle.
🛠️Remediation
Root cause analysis is completed within 5 business days. Findings are used to harden our controls and prevent similar incidents in the future.

🐛 Responsible Disclosure

If you've discovered a potential security vulnerability in Zenith, we want to hear from you. We have a responsible disclosure program and will work with you to address verified issues promptly.

1
Report the issue
Email a detailed description of the vulnerability to production@fortumars.com with the subject line "Security Disclosure".
2
We acknowledge within 24 hours
Our security team will confirm receipt and provide a timeline for investigation.
3
Investigation and fix
We'll investigate, develop a fix, and keep you updated throughout the process.
4
Public acknowledgment
With your permission, we'll credit you in our security acknowledgments once the issue is resolved.
