Track the KPIs that matter most

MarsCart tracks 40+ retail KPIs out of the box. Here are the most popular ones.

💰
Revenue & GMV
Total gross merchandise value, net revenue, and revenue by channel (POS vs. online) across any time period.
🔄
Sell-Through Rate
Percentage of your inventory that sold in a given period. Essential for buying decisions and end-of-season markdowns.
🛒
Average Order Value
Average spend per transaction, broken down by channel, branch, time of day, and customer segment.
🔁
Customer Retention
What percentage of your customers came back for a second purchase? Tracked by cohort and purchase frequency.
📦
Inventory Turnover
How many times your inventory sells and is replaced in a period. A key indicator of buying efficiency.
↩️
Return Rate
Percentage of items returned, broken down by product, category, channel, and reason code.
