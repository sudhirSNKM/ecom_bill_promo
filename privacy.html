Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2026 · Last Updated: March 1, 2026

Zenith ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy of boutique owners, their staff, and their customers who use the Zenith platform. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your information.

By using the Zenith platform, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly, as well as data generated by your use of the platform:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number, company name, and billing details when you sign up.
  • Transaction Data: Sales records, invoice data, payment amounts, and product details processed through the POS system or e-commerce storefront.
  • Inventory Data: Product catalog, stock levels, supplier information, and transfer history.
  • Customer Data (on behalf of merchants): Customer names, emails, purchase histories, wishlists, and delivery addresses collected by merchants using Zenith's e-commerce and CRM features.
  • Usage Data: Log files, IP addresses, browser type, pages visited, session duration, and other analytics data collected automatically.
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, and hardware identifiers for mobile and POS terminal access.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide, operate, and improve the Zenith platform and its features.
  • To process transactions and send related receipts and invoices.
  • To send administrative communications, including service updates, security alerts, and support messages.
  • To perform analytics to understand usage patterns and improve the product.
  • To comply with legal obligations, including tax and financial reporting requirements.
  • To detect, prevent, and respond to fraud, abuse, and security incidents.

3. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal data. We may share it in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: We engage trusted vendors (e.g., cloud infrastructure, payment processors, analytics providers) who process data only on our behalf and under strict confidentiality obligations.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose data when required by law, court order, or government authority.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, customer data may be transferred as part of the transaction.

4. Cookies and Tracking

We use essential cookies to operate the platform (session management, security). We also use analytics cookies to understand how users interact with our product. You can control cookie preferences in your browser settings.

5. Security

We implement AES-256 encryption at rest, TLS 1.3 in transit, multi-factor authentication, and routine penetration testing to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to: access your personal data, correct inaccurate data, request deletion of your data, restrict or object to processing, and request a portable copy of your data. To exercise these rights, contact us at production@fortumars.com.

7. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. Transaction records are retained for a minimum of 7 years for financial compliance. You may request deletion at any time, subject to legal retention requirements.

8. Children's Privacy

Zenith is a business platform and is not directed at individuals under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via email or a notice within the platform at least 14 days before they take effect.

10. Contact

For privacy-related questions or to exercise your rights, contact our Data Protection team at production@fortumars.com.
